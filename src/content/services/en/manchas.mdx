Dark spots take away the brightness and even tone of your face. The **dark spot treatment in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa combines technology with depigmenting active ingredients to brighten the skin safely and progressively.

## Why dark spots appear

Hyperpigmentation occurs when the skin produces excess melanin in certain areas. The most common causes are unprotected sun exposure, hormonal changes (such as pregnancy melasma), age, and the aftermath of acne or injuries.

Understanding the origin of the spot is essential: not all of them respond to the same treatment.

## How we treat dark spots

After an evaluation to identify the type of spot, we design a protocol that may combine:

- Professional depigmenting active ingredients that regulate melanin production.
- Laser technology and resurfacing to renew pigmented skin.
- Activated charcoal with laser to brighten and improve texture.
- Home care and sun protection to sustain results.

## Benefits

- A more even and luminous skin tone.
- Visible reduction of dark spots and localized pigmentation.
- Renewed skin with better texture.
- Protocols tailored to each type of spot and skin.

## The importance of sun protection

No dark spot treatment is complete without daily sun protection. The sun is the main reason spots reappear, which is why we make it an essential part of your plan.
