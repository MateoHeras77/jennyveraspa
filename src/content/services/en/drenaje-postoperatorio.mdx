Recovery is as important as the surgery itself. **Post-operative lymphatic drainage in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa supports you in reducing swelling, preventing complications, and enhancing the results of your procedure.

## What post-operative lymphatic drainage is

It is a gentle, specialized massage technique that stimulates the lymphatic system to move the fluid that builds up after surgery. This reduces swelling, relieves discomfort, and promotes a faster, safer recovery.

## Why it matters after surgery

After a procedure such as liposuction or abdominoplasty, the body retains fluid and starts a normal inflammatory process. Without proper management, this can prolong recovery or encourage fibrosis. Drainage helps to:

- Reduce swelling and the feeling of heaviness.
- Prevent and treat fibrosis and hardened areas.
- Improve circulation and tissue oxygenation.
- Relieve discomfort and speed up recovery.
- Optimize the final aesthetic result of the surgery.

## What the treatment is like

### Initial evaluation

We review the type of surgery, the time elapsed, and your surgeon's guidance to design a safe protocol.

### Drainage sessions

We apply gentle, rhythmic maneuvers in the direction of the lymphatic system. The first sessions tend to be more frequent and are then spaced out based on your progress.

### Follow-up

We monitor your progress session by session, adjusting the plan and providing home-care recommendations.

## We work alongside your surgeon

Our approach complements medical guidance. We coordinate the start and frequency of sessions with your surgeon's recommendation for an optimal recovery.
