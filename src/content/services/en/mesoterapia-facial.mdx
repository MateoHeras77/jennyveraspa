Restoring radiance and comfort to the skin is possible with a deep supply of nutrients. The **facial mesotherapy in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa introduces selected actives to hydrate, revitalize, and improve the quality of your face with a protocol tailored to your skin type.

## What facial mesotherapy is

It is a treatment that introduces vitamins, minerals, and other actives into the superficial layers of the skin through microinjections. Unlike a topical cream, it delivers nutrients exactly where they are needed, promoting more hydrated, radiant, and better-textured skin from within.

## Phases of the treatment

### Evaluation and diagnosis

We assess your skin type and goals to define the protocol and the combination of actives best suited to you.

### Preparation and application

We prepare the area and perform the microinjections in a controlled way, distributing the actives evenly across the face.

### Aftercare

We finish with hydration and protection guidelines to care for the skin and enhance results in the following days.

## Benefits

- Deep hydration and immediate comfort.
- Improved radiance and appearance of dull skin.
- Promotes more uniform texture and tone.
- Helps soften early signs of aging.
- Easily integrated into non-surgical rejuvenation plans.

## Who it is for

Facial mesotherapy is ideal for dehydrated, dull, or early-aging skin that wants to revitalize. It is also an excellent complement within an ongoing facial plan to maintain skin quality over time.
