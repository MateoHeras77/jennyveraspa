If you have moles that bother you for aesthetic reasons or because of their location, the **laser mole removal in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa offers a safe, precise procedure with minimal scarring.

## How laser removal works

The laser acts in a controlled way on the mole, removing the tissue layer by layer with great precision while protecting the surrounding healthy skin. This results in more discreet scarring than with other methods.

## The evaluation is the first step

Before removing any mole, we carry out an evaluation. If the mole shows characteristics that require further study (color changes, irregular borders, growth), we recommend a prior dermatological evaluation. Safety always comes first.

> Never remove a suspicious mole without a professional evaluation. Knowing the warning signs is essential.

## Benefits

- A precise, minimally invasive procedure.
- Minimal scarring compared to other methods.
- Short sessions and fast recovery.
- A clean, natural aesthetic result.

## What to expect

### During the session

After preparing and applying local anesthesia to the area, we apply the laser to the mole. The procedure is quick and well tolerated.

### After the treatment

A small scab forms and falls off naturally within a few days. We provide you with care instructions and sun protection guidance for optimal healing.

## Aftercare

Keeping the area clean, not picking at the scab, and applying sun protection are key to a flawless result. We guide you through every step of the process.
