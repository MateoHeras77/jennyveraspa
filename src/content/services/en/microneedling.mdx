The **microneedling in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa awakens the regenerative power of your own skin. With Dermapen technology we stimulate collagen production to renew texture, soften scars, and restore radiance, without destroying tissue.

## What microneedling is

Microneedling is a procedure that uses ultra-fine needles, at a depth of between 0.5 and 2.5 mm depending on the need, to create controlled microchannels in the skin. These stimuli activate the body's natural repair system and trigger the production of new collagen and elastin.

We use the Dermapen, a motorized device considered the gold standard for its precision: it vibrates rapidly and allows adjusting depth, speed, and coverage to achieve predictable results that are superior to the manual roller.

## Benefits of the treatment

- Stimulates the production of your own collagen and elastin.
- Softens acne scars and skin depressions.
- Contracts enlarged pores and evens out texture.
- Smooths fine lines and improves firmness.
- Restores radiance to dull, tired skin.

## Who it is for

Microneedling is ideal if you have scars, enlarged pores, uneven texture, or fine lines, or if you are looking for natural, progressive rejuvenation before turning to more invasive procedures.

### How it is applied

After an evaluation, we prepare the skin and apply the Dermapen in systematic patterns over the area to be treated. The session lasts between 20 and 30 minutes, is comfortable, and requires no downtime.

### Results

Improvement is progressive: the skin renews itself over the 8 to 12 weeks following each session, and results accumulate with the full protocol. Unlike a peeling or a laser, we do not destroy tissue but awaken your own skin's healing power, so the changes are natural and long-lasting.

## Combining with exosomes or PRP

This is where microneedling shines. The open microchannels allow regenerative actives to penetrate much deeper. That is why we combine it with [exosomes](/en/servicios/exosomas) or [platelet-rich plasma](/en/servicios/plasma-rico-plaquetas), enhancing tissue repair and achieving superior, longer-lasting results in fewer sessions.
