## What facial lymphatic drainage is

It is a gentle, specialized manual massage technique that stimulates the face's lymphatic system to move the fluid and waste that build up and congest the skin. The lymphatic system works like your skin's natural cleansing network, and when its flow slows down, puffiness, dark circles, and a dull look appear. By reactivating it with precise maneuvers, we reduce inflammation, relieve discomfort, and promote cleaner, firmer, and more radiant skin.

## Why it helps with puffiness

Stress, lack of sleep, pollution, and the passage of time slow down the face's lymphatic flow, causing fluid retention and a dull appearance. Drainage helps to:

- Reduce facial puffiness and under-eye bags.
- Soften dark circles and give a rested look.
- Improve circulation and skin oxygenation.
- Support toxin removal and facial definition.
- Relieve facial tension and provide deep relaxation.

### Initial evaluation

### Follow-up

We monitor your progress session by session, adjusting the plan and providing home-care recommendations.
