Regenerative medicine allows the skin to rejuvenate from its own biology. The **salmon PDRN treatment in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa stimulates cellular regeneration to achieve firmer, more hydrated, and more luminous skin, without surgery and naturally.

## What salmon PDRN is

PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide) is a regenerative molecule derived from salmon DNA. Purified and standardized under rigorous quality controls, it is applied to the skin to send biological signals that activate cellular repair.

Unlike hyaluronic acid, which hydrates on the surface, PDRN works deep down: it instructs your skin to repair and renew itself from within.

## Benefits of the treatment

- Stimulate cellular regeneration and collagen production.
- Improve deep hydration, firmness, and elasticity.
- Soften fine lines and signs of fatigue.
- Help repair damaged or sensitized skin.
- Add radiance and a more even texture.

## Who it is for

Salmon PDRN is ideal if you are looking for natural rejuvenation, have dull, dehydrated skin or early signs of aging, or want to enhance the results of other regenerative protocols.

### How it is applied

After an evaluation, we prepare the skin and apply the PDRN to the area to be treated. The procedure is comfortable, quick, and requires no downtime, so you can resume your day as normal.

### Results

The skin looks progressively more hydrated, firm, and revitalized as cellular regeneration advances. The result is natural and is enhanced with the full protocol.

## PDRN, exosomes, or PRP

All three are regenerative treatments, but they work differently. PDRN repairs and hydrates at the cellular level; [exosomes](/en/servicios/exosomas) transmit renewal signals between cells; and [platelet-rich plasma](/en/servicios/plasma-rico-plaquetas) harnesses the growth factors from your own blood. They are often combined to multiply the results.
