Get defined, natural-looking brows without relying on daily makeup. **Microblading in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa is a semi-permanent micropigmentation technique that draws hair-like strokes to frame your eyes with a harmonious finish tailored to your face.

## What microblading is

Microblading places pigment in the superficial layers of the skin through very fine strokes that imitate the natural hairs of the eyebrow. The result is fuller, more symmetrical, and better-defined brows with a realistic look. Before we begin, we design the shape according to your features, the symmetry of your face, and the effect you want, so every stroke blends in naturally.

## Benefits

- More defined, fuller, and symmetrical brows.
- A natural look thanks to the hair-stroke effect.
- Less makeup time in your daily routine.
- A semi-permanent result that lasts between one and two years.
- A personalized design based on the shape of your face.

## Who it is for

Microblading is ideal if you have sparse brows, areas thinned by over-plucking, or gaps with no hair, or if you simply want a more defined frame for your eyes. It is a great option for anyone looking to save makeup time and keep neat, polished brows all day long.

### Healing and aftercare

During the first days the brows look bolder and small scabs may form; this is a normal part of the process. Keep the area clean and dry, avoid direct sun, do not scratch or remove the scabs, and follow the aftercare instructions we provide. The skin finishes settling the color in about four weeks, and the initial touch-up fine-tunes the final result.
