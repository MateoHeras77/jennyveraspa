Healthy skin starts with a deep, professional cleansing. The **deep facial cleansing in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa purifies, renews, and brightens your face with a protocol tailored to your skin type.

## What deep facial cleansing is

It is a treatment that removes impurities, dead cells, excess oil, and comedones accumulated in the pores. Unlike a home cleanse, it is performed in phases with professional products and technology to achieve truly clean, oxygenated skin.

## Phases of the treatment

### Diagnosis and makeup removal

We assess your skin type and remove makeup and surface impurities to prepare the face.

### Exfoliation and extraction

We remove dead cells and, when necessary, perform a controlled extraction of comedones to safely unclog pores.

### Hydration and protection

We finish with soothing actives, deep hydration, and protection to seal in the results and leave the skin radiant.

## Benefits

- Clean, less visible pores.
- Improved texture and radiance from the first session.
- Helps control acne and excess oil.
- Prepares the skin to better absorb other treatments.
- Immediate sensation of freshness and cleanliness.

## Who it is for

Deep facial cleansing is ideal for all skin types, especially skin with clogged pores, oily tendencies, blackheads, or a dull appearance. It is also an excellent first step before starting other facial protocols.
