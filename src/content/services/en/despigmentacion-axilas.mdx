## Why underarms get darker

Underarm darkening is a very common form of hyperpigmentation that affects women of all ages and skin tones. Its most frequent causes are repeated waxing or shaving, which creates microtrauma and chronic irritation; constant friction from tight clothing; the use of aggressive deodorants and antiperspirants; the hormonal changes of pregnancy or birth control; and inflammation from reactions to products.

Understanding the origin is essential: not all cases respond the same, which is why every protocol starts with an evaluation where we assess the type of pigmentation and the most likely cause.
