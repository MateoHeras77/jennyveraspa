**Intimate HIFU in Cuenca** is a non-invasive treatment for female intimate rejuvenation, designed to improve tissue firmness, comfort, and intimate health without surgery. At Jenny Vera Spa we perform it with focused-ultrasound technology, in a completely private setting and with professional guidance.

## What intimate HIFU is

HIFU stands for high-intensity focused ultrasound. In the intimate context, the energy is concentrated in the deep layers of the tissue to generate controlled heat that stimulates natural collagen and elastin production, without incisions or general anesthesia.

This regeneration process strengthens and tones the tissue gradually, improving its firmness and elasticity. It is a clinical procedure that works with the physiology of the skin and mucosa, not a surgical intervention.

## Benefits of intimate HIFU

- Improves the firmness and elasticity of intimate tissue.
- Contributes to greater comfort in daily life and in intimate relations.
- Supports natural lubrication in cases of dryness.
- Helps tone the area after postpartum or with the changes of age.
- Requires no surgery, general anesthesia, or prolonged downtime.

## Who it is for

Intimate HIFU is indicated for women who notice loss of firmness, dryness, discomfort, or changes in the intimate area associated with postpartum, menopause, or the passage of time. It is also an option for those who prefer to avoid surgical procedures. During the evaluation we confirm it is the right choice for your case.

### What to expect in your session

We start with a private, confidential evaluation to understand your needs and answer your questions without taboos. During the session we apply the device handpiece with controlled pulses; most patients describe a mild, well-tolerated sensation of warmth. The session lasts approximately thirty to forty-five minutes.

### Aftercare

It requires no recovery: you can return to your routine the same day. We recommend avoiding intimate relations and very hot baths during the first forty-eight to seventy-two hours, and following our team's instructions.

## Results and maintenance

Results appear progressively as the tissue builds new collagen, with an improvement that usually consolidates over the following weeks and lasts for months. An initial protocol of several sessions plus periodic maintenance extends the effect.
