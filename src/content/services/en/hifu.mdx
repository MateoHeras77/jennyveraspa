If you want to redefine your facial contour and restore firmness without surgery, **HIFU in Cuenca** is one of the most requested treatments at Jenny Vera Spa. Using HIFU 360 Max technology, we work the deep layers of the skin to stimulate collagen and achieve a natural, progressive lifting effect.

## What HIFU is

HIFU stands for high-intensity focused ultrasound. It is a non-invasive technology that concentrates ultrasound waves in the deep layers of the skin, generating controlled heat exactly where laxity occurs, without damaging the surface.

Unlike treatments that only act on the epidermis, HIFU reaches the muscular layer (SMAS), the same one a surgeon tightens in a traditional facelift. That is why it delivers a real tightening effect without incisions.

## Benefits of HIFU

- Tightens and firms the face, neck, and jawline.
- Stimulates natural collagen and elastin production.
- Defines the facial oval and softens a double chin.
- Requires no surgery, general anesthesia, or downtime.
- Natural, progressive results that last over time.

## Who it is for

HIFU is ideal if you notice loss of firmness, mild to moderate sagging, or a less defined facial contour. It is also an excellent option for those who want to prevent the signs of aging without invasive procedures.

### What to expect in your session

We start with an evaluation to assess your skin and define the areas to treat. During the session we apply the device handpiece over the prepared skin, adjusting the depth for each area. The session lasts approximately forty to sixty minutes.

### Aftercare

HIFU has no recovery time: you can return to your routine the same day. We recommend staying well hydrated, using sun protection, and avoiding intense heat sources during the first few hours.

## Results and maintenance

You will notice an immediate sense of firmness, but the full result appears gradually over the following two to three months, as your skin builds new collagen. To maintain the effect, we suggest one session per year.
