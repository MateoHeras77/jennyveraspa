## What relaxing massages are

These are full-body massages with gentle, flowing, slow-paced techniques that work on the muscles and the nervous system. Unlike other massages, the goal is not to reduce measurements but to calm the mind, let go of built-up tension, and bring back a feeling of rest. We work areas such as the back, neck, shoulders, and legs with warm oils.
