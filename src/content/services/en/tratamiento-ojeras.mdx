The eye area is one of the most delicate parts of the face and often reveals tiredness, age, or lack of rest even when you sleep well. The **under-eye treatment in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa starts with a professional diagnosis to improve dark circles, hollowing, and the texture of the periocular area with a plan tailored to you.

## What the under-eye treatment is

It is a personalized protocol designed to reduce dark circles based on their origin. Not all dark circles are the same: they can be caused by pigmentation, local circulation, volume loss, or skin laxity. That is why the first step is always to assess which component predominates in order to choose the safest, most effective approach for each person.

## Phases of the treatment

### Eye area diagnosis

We assess the type of dark circle, the condition of the skin, and the habits that influence its appearance to set realistic goals and a plan tailored to you.

### Personalized protocol

We apply the most suitable care for your case, aimed at improving the hydration, radiance, and firmness of the periocular area gradually and safely.

### Aftercare and follow-up

We finish with at-home recommendations and a session plan that helps maintain and enhance the results over time.

## Benefits

- A brighter, more rested eye area.
- Gradual improvement in color and texture.
- More hydrated, firmer eyelid skin.
- A plan adapted to each person's type of dark circle.
- Care recommendations to prolong the result.

## Who it is for

The under-eye treatment is ideal for those who notice a tired look, with dark coloring, puffiness, or a dull contour. It is also for those who want to preventively care for an area as exposed and delicate as the periocular one. Each plan is adjusted after assessing the predominant type of dark circle.
