Regenerative medicine harnesses your own body's resources to care for your skin. The **PRP treatment in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa uses a concentrated fraction of your blood to stimulate natural regeneration for firmer, more even, and more luminous skin. ## What PRP is

Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) is a biostimulation treatment that uses components of your own blood. A concentrated fraction of platelets and growth factors is obtained and then applied to the face in a controlled way.

Because it is biocompatible, it works in harmony with your skin to activate its own repair and renewal mechanisms.

## Benefits of the treatment

- Stimulates cellular regeneration and collagen production.
- Improves firmness, elasticity, and radiance.
- Evens out texture and revitalizes the skin's appearance.
- Helps soften fine lines and small irregularities.
- Enhances skin recovery after other procedures.

## Who it is for

PRP facial is ideal if you are looking for natural, biocompatible rejuvenation, want to improve the quality of your skin, or wish to support firmness without relying on external products. By using your own growth factors, it is very well tolerated.

### What to expect in your session

After an evaluation in which we assess your skin and goals, we process the sample to concentrate the platelets and apply the PRP to the target areas with a safe protocol. The procedure is comfortable and requires no downtime. Afterward, we provide recommendations to support recovery.

The skin looks more even and revitalized progressively, as regeneration advances, and the result is enhanced with the full protocol.
