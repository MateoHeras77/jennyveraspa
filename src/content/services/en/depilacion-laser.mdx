Forget the razor and wax. **Laser hair removal in Cuenca** with diode laser technology lets you reduce unwanted hair progressively, comfortably, and lastingly at Jenny Vera Spa.

## How laser hair removal works

The diode laser emits a beam of light that is absorbed by the melanin in the hair. That energy turns into heat and disables the follicle in its growth phase, progressively reducing the amount and thickness of hair session after session.

The device includes a cooling system that protects the skin and makes the experience much more comfortable than traditional methods.

## Benefits of the diode laser

- Progressive, long-lasting hair reduction.
- Suitable for multiple face and body areas.
- Fast, comfortable sessions thanks to cooling.
- Improves skin appearance and prevents ingrown hairs.
- Visible results from the first sessions.

## Areas we treat

We perform laser hair removal on the underarms, legs, bikini area, face, back, arms, and other areas, for both women and men. Each area has a specific protocol based on hair type and density.

### What to expect in your session

After an initial evaluation, we prepare the area and glide the laser handpiece over the skin. Duration depends on the area: a few minutes for underarms and longer for full legs.

### Before and after care

Before the session, avoid intense sun exposure and do not wax or pluck (the laser needs the hair root). Afterward, apply sun protection and stay hydrated. We provide clear instructions at every visit.

## Results

You will notice visible reduction from the first sessions. Lasting removal requires completing the protocol, since hair grows in different cycles and the laser acts on those that are active.
