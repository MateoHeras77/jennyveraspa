## What botox is

Botox is the application of botulinum toxin in controlled doses to relax the muscles responsible for dynamic lines, the ones that appear when you make facial expressions. It does not erase your gestures or freeze your face: well dosed, it softens the lines and helps prevent them from deepening over time. It is a clinical procedure that requires an individual facial diagnosis, precise technique, and follow-up.
