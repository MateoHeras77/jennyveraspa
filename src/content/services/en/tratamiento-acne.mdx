Acne and oily skin can affect the texture, tone, and confidence of your skin. The **acne treatment in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa combines deep cleansing, oil control, and personalized protocols to reduce breakouts and restore balance to your skin.

## How we treat acne

Every skin is different, so we start with a complete diagnosis to design the right protocol.

### Deep cleansing

We unclog pores, remove impurities, and perform a controlled extraction of comedones to reduce inflammation and prevent new breakouts.

### Oily skin control

We treat excess sebum with specific actives that regulate oil production, soothe the skin, and improve its overall appearance.

### Personalized protocols

We adjust products, frequency, and care to your acne type, age, and skin sensitivity, with professional follow-up in every session.

## Benefits

- Reduces active acne breakouts.
- Controls excess oil and shine.
- Unclogs and refines pores.
- Improves skin texture and radiance.
- Prevents new lesions with a consistent routine.

## Who it is for

It is ideal for teenagers with hormonal acne, adults with persistent acne, and people with oily or combination skin looking for more balanced skin. It is also an excellent starting point before treating marks or scars.

### Acne scars

When acne has left marks or scars, we work on texture and tone evenness with specific protocols such as fractional CO2 laser. Explore all of our [services](/en/servicios) and learn more in our article about [CO2 laser for acne scars](/en/blog/laser-co2-cicatrices-acne).

### What to expect

Many people notice cleaner, fresher skin from the first session. Controlling active acne usually requires a plan of several sessions, with at-home care that we reinforce at each visit to maintain results over the medium term.
