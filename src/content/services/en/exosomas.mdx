## What exosomes are

Exosomes are microscopic vesicles that cells use to communicate with one another. They carry growth factors, proteins, and signals that activate regeneration. Applied to the skin, they instruct it to repair and renew itself.

It is one of the most advanced technologies in rejuvenation, because it works at the cellular level and not only on the surface.

## Benefits of the treatment

- Stimulate cellular renewal and collagen production.
- Improve firmness, elasticity, and radiance.
- Soften fine lines and signs of fatigue.
- Even out skin tone and improve texture.
- Enhance recovery after other procedures.

## Who it is for

The exosome treatment is ideal if you are looking for natural rejuvenation, want to improve the quality of your skin, or wish to enhance the results of other protocols such as microneedling or HIFU.
