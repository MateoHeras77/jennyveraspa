The **laser carbon peel in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa, also known as the Hollywood Peel, deeply cleanses your skin, refines pores, and delivers instant radiance with no downtime.

## What the laser carbon peel is

It is a non-invasive facial treatment that combines an activated charcoal mask with laser energy. The charcoal penetrates the pores and absorbs impurities, oil, and toxins, acting like a magnet that captures accumulated pollution. The laser then vaporizes it and carries away surface dead cells while stimulating collagen production. It is one of the preferred protocols for achieving a far deeper cleanse than a conventional facial, with an instant luminous finish.

## Phases of the treatment

### Diagnosis and cleansing

We assess your skin type and perform a prior cleanse to prepare the face and enhance the results.

### Activated charcoal application

We spread the charcoal mask over the clean skin and let it dry so it penetrates the pores and captures impurities.

### Laser activation

We apply fast, painless laser pulses that vaporize the charcoal, remove impurities, and stimulate cell regeneration. You feel only a gentle warmth throughout the session. We finish with soothing actives and sun protection so the skin recovers comfortably and stays radiant.

## Benefits

- Deep pore cleansing and control of excess oil.
- Visible reduction in pore size.
- Improved skin texture and tone.
- Collagen stimulation for a firmer appearance.
- Instant radiance from the first session, with no downtime.

## Who it is for

The Hollywood Peel is ideal for oily or combination skin with enlarged pores, acne tendency, uneven texture, or a dull appearance. It is also an excellent option for achieving a luminous glow before an important event, since it reveals a more rested face with no need for downtime. During the evaluation we analyze your skin and define how many sessions you need and how often to schedule them to maintain the results over time.
