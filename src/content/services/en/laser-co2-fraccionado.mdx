If you want to renew your skin in depth, the **fractional CO2 laser in Cuenca** at Jenny Vera Spa offers precise resurfacing to improve acne scars, wrinkles and texture with a controlled recovery.

## How fractional CO2 laser resurfacing works

The laser emits microcolumns of infrared light that vaporize small fractions of skin in a controlled way, leaving the surrounding tissue intact. This "controlled injury" activates your fibroblasts and stimulates new collagen production, which remodels the skin from the deepest layers.

## Precision that respects your lifestyle

By treating the skin in fractions rather than the entire surface at once, recovery is much faster than with the fully ablative CO2 laser. You get deep results with fewer days of social downtime.

> Renewal does not happen in a single day: new collagen keeps building for weeks after each session.

## Benefits

- Visible improvement of atrophic acne scars.
- Facial rejuvenation: softens wrinkles and expression lines.
- Smoother texture, refined pores and more even tone.
- Progressive, long-lasting results thanks to your own collagen.

## What to expect

### During the session

We apply topical anesthesia before starting so the procedure is well tolerated. We then pass the laser over the area to be treated; the session is quick and precise.

### After the treatment

The first few days the skin looks red and shows fine flaking that resolves within a few days. From there you will see more renewed skin that keeps improving over the following weeks.

## Aftercare

SPF 50+ sun protection is your best ally, along with plenty of hydration and not picking at the flaking. We provide you with clear instructions and guide you through every stage for an optimal result.
