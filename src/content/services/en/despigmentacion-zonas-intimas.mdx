Hyperpigmentation in the intimate area is more common than most people think and affects women of all ages and skin tones. ## Why intimate hyperpigmentation appears

Darkening of the intimate area is a natural skin response to different factors. The most common causes are constant friction from tight clothing, repeated hair removal with wax or shaving that creates microtrauma and irritation, hormonal changes from pregnancy or contraceptives, chronic inflammation, and genetic predisposition.

Understanding the origin is essential: not all cases respond the same way, which is why every protocol starts with an evaluation where we analyze the type of pigmentation and the most likely cause, always in a confidential setting.

## How we treat intimate brightening

After assessing the type of pigmentation and your skin type, we design a personalized protocol that may combine:

- Specialized laser technology that fragments and renews pigmented layers, with safe options even on darker skin.
- Professional depigmenting active ingredients that regulate melanin production.
- Guidance to change hair removal habits, friction, and irritating products that perpetuate darkening.
- Home care and protection to sustain the lightening between sessions.

During each session we monitor the skin's response and adjust the intensity to achieve natural, gradual results, always prioritizing your comfort and privacy.

No brightening treatment is complete without changing the habits that darken the skin. Reducing friction, choosing gentle hair removal, using non-irritating products, and protecting the area are an essential part of your plan so your results last.
