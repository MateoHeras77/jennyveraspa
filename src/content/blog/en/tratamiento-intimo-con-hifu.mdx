## What is Intimate HIFU?

HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) is technology that:

### How It Works

- **Emits ultrasound**: High-frequency focused waves
- **Penetrates tissues**: Reaches deep layers of collagen
- **Generates controlled heat**: Stimulates collagen regeneration
- **Naturally tones**: Your body responds by producing new collagen and elastin

**It's like a "non-invasive lift" for the intimate area.**

### Why It's Different

- **Non-invasive**: No cuts or stitches
- **No anesthesia**: Not necessary
- **No recovery**: Return home same day
- **Natural**: Your body does the regeneration
- **Long-lasting**: Results improve over time

It's recommended to wait 48-72 hours after each session to allow tissues to adapt.

Yes, but the session is more comfortable after your period. You can schedule according to preference.

### Can my partner notice the difference?

### Is it permanent?

It's not surgically permanent, but results last 12-18 months. Annual maintenance maintains benefits indefinitely.
