Facial HIFU is a non-invasive option to improve skin firmness and contour without surgery. It is often chosen by people who want progressive tightening with minimal interruption to their routine.

## What is facial HIFU

HIFU stands for High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound. The energy targets deeper layers to stimulate collagen without damaging the skin surface.

## Most requested benefits

### Better firmness

It helps redefine areas with mild to moderate skin laxity.

### Visible softening of fine lines

By improving overall skin quality, it can smooth early signs of aging.

### Non-surgical procedure

No incisions and no long recovery periods are required.

## How a session is performed

### 1. Facial assessment

Your specialist confirms if you are a candidate and maps treatment zones.

### 2. Focused energy application

Energy is delivered through vectors to stimulate deeper tissue layers.

### 3. Follow-up and aftercare

You receive practical care recommendations to protect and maximize results.

## Who is it recommended for

It is commonly recommended for people with early or moderate laxity who want improved firmness without surgery.
