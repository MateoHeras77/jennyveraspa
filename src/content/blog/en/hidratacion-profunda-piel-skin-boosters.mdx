## Why Creams Aren't Enough

Skin has multiple layers. Moisturizing creams work primarily on the epidermis (outer layer), but when there is deep dehydration, persistent spots, advanced wrinkles, or loss of elasticity, the problem lies in deeper layers: the dermis.

In the dermis live fibroblasts, the cells responsible for producing collagen and elastin. As years pass, the production of these proteins diminishes, and no serum or cream can reverse this from the outside alone.

That's why treatments that stimulate regeneration from within are the ones that truly work. Our team at [Jenny Vera Spa presents the most advanced solutions](/en/servicios) to transform your skin from the root.

## What Are Skin Boosters

Skin boosters are micro-injections of hydrating substances and active ingredients deposited in the mid-dermis. Unlike fillers that add volume, boosters hydrate and stimulate natural collagen production.

Common components include:

- Hyaluronic acid (retains hydration)
- Vitamins (A, C, E)
- Antioxidants
- Growth factors
- Collagen stimulators

### How They Work in Your Skin

1. **Immediate hydration:** hyaluronic acid retains water in deep layers.
2. **Gradual stimulation:** active ingredients activate fibroblasts to produce new collagen.
3. **Progressive results:** skin improves week by week over 4-6 weeks.

### Duration of Results

Initial results appear within 1-2 weeks. Peak improvement is reached between 4-6 weeks. Duration depends on the product and your metabolism, but maintenance is generally recommended every 6-12 months.

## Exosomes for Hydration: How They Work

Exosomes are extracellular vesicles produced by stem cells. They are messenger molecules that transport proteins, lipids, and nucleic acids to communicate instructions to other cells.

When applied topically or injected into the dermis, exosomes:

- **Reprogram** fibroblasts to produce more collagen and elastin.
- **Reduce** chronic skin inflammation.
- **Improve** overall skin quality at the cellular level.
- **Potentiate** other treatments like mesotherapy or microneedling.

### Exosomes vs Moisturizing Creams

The fundamental difference is the mechanism: a cream hydrates the surface; exosomes communicate at the cellular level so your skin regenerates from within.

Recent studies show that exosomes can improve hydration, radiance, elasticity, and reduce expression lines significantly within 4-8 weeks.

## PDRN: Hydration + Regeneration Simultaneously

PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide) is a natural salmon DNA extract that functions as a "regeneration code" for your skin cells.

### How PDRN Works

PDRN activates:

- **A2A receptors:** stimulate collagen and elastin production.
- **Growth factors:** accelerate cellular repair.
- **Vascular regeneration:** improves blood flow and skin oxygenation.
- **Cellular energy:** increases ATP levels in cells.

### Visible Benefits of PDRN

- Firmer and more elastic skin within 2-3 weeks.
- Visible improvement in atrophic scars and expression lines.
- Natural radiance and more rested appearance.
- Duration: 4-6 months per session.
