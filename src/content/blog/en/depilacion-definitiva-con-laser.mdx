## What is Diode Laser Hair Removal?

The diode laser is an innovative technology that uses concentrated near-infrared light to destroy the hair follicle at its root. Unlike other methods, diode laser is particularly effective because:

### How Diode Laser Works

The procedure is simple but highly effective:

1. **Selective absorption**: The laser emits light that is absorbed by melanin (pigment) in the hair follicle.
2. **Heat generation**: This heat permanently destroys hair-producing cells.
3. **Skin protection**: Integrated cooling technology protects surrounding skin.

The result: permanently damaged hair follicles, without affecting the healthy skin that surrounds them.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Diode Laser Hair Removal

### Is it safe for all skin types?

Yes. Diode laser is versatile but requires adjustments based on your skin type. Our professionals evaluate your skin before each session to ensure maximum safety.

### Does it hurt?

Most describe the sensation as uncomfortable but tolerable. If you have low pain sensitivity, we offer topical numbing cream options.

### Is there recovery time?

There is no downtime. You can resume normal activities immediately, although it is recommended to avoid direct sun for 48 hours.

### What should I do after treatment?

- Avoid direct sun for 48 hours
- Don't use antiperspirant deodorants on treated armpits for 24 hours
- Use SPF 30+ if you go outside
- Avoid pools/jacuzzis for 24 hours

### Does hair grow back?

With diode laser hair removal, hair generally does not return completely in treated areas. Some follicles may reactivate due to hormonal changes over the years, but most remain inactive permanently.
