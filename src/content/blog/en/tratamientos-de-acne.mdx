Acne can affect skin texture, tone, and confidence. A professional approach helps treat active lesions and residual marks more safely and with better medium-term outcomes.

## What acne is and why it appears

Acne is a multifactorial condition linked to excess sebum, clogged pores, bacteria, and inflammation. Hormones, stress, and skin-care habits can also influence flare-ups.

## Most common professional treatments

### Deep facial cleansing

Helps remove impurities and improve overall skin condition.

### Controlled chemical peels

Promote skin renewal and can improve superficial marks.

### Laser and light technologies

Used to address scars, texture, and skin tone uniformity.

### Facial mesotherapy

Delivers active ingredients that support hydration and skin recovery.

## Habits that help control breakouts

- Gentle cleansing twice a day.
- Avoid squeezing pimples.
- Use non-comedogenic products.
- Keep a consistent routine.
