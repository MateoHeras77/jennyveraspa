Laser treatments can address multiple aesthetic needs with precision, from hair reduction to pigmentation concerns, texture refinement, and visible signs of aging.

## What can be treated with laser

### Laser hair reduction

Used to progressively reduce unwanted hair.

### Facial rejuvenation

Some laser technologies stimulate collagen and improve firmness.

### Dark spots and marks

Selected devices can help improve pigmentation and skin tone uniformity.

## Benefits of a personalized treatment plan

- Technology selected according to your skin type.
- Parameters adjusted for safety and effectiveness.
- Follow-up to evaluate response and optimize settings.

## Basic before and after care

### Before treatment

Avoid intense sun exposure and follow your specialist's pre-care instructions.

### After treatment

Use sunscreen, keep skin hydrated, and respect the interval between sessions.
