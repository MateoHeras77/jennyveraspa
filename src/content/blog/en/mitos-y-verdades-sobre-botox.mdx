Botox is one of the most requested procedures in facial aesthetics. It is also one of the most misunderstood. Understanding the basics helps you make informed decisions with confidence.

## Myth: it will freeze your face

**Fact:** when correctly dosed, Botox does not erase your expression. It softens dynamic lines and helps prevent them from becoming deeper over time.

The key factors are:

- Personalized facial assessment.
- Correct dosage per area.
- Precise injection technique.

## Myth: it is only for older patients

**Fact:** it can also be used preventively in younger adults with strong facial movement or early lines.

It is not only about chronological age; it is about anatomy, lifestyle, and aesthetic goals.

## Myth: repeating treatment is unsafe

**Fact:** in trained hands and with approved products, Botox has a strong safety profile. Frequency should be decided during follow-up according to duration and individual response.

## How long does the effect last

On average, results last around 3 to 5 months, although this can vary by metabolism, technique, and treated area. It is best to avoid overcorrection and respect re-evaluation timing.

## Signs of a professional consultation

Look for providers who prioritize:

- Complete medical assessment.
- Personalized treatment plan.
- Post-treatment follow-up.
- Natural, balanced outcomes.

## Practical conclusion

Botox is not magic, and it is not automatically risky. It is a clinical tool that works very well when diagnosis, technique, and follow-up are done correctly.

Your best decision is to stay informed and choose a team that combines aesthetics with medical criteria.
