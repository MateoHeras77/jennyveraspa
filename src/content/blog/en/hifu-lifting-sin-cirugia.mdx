If you want to improve facial firmness without surgery, HIFU is one of the most interesting technologies in modern aesthetic medicine.

## What is HIFU and why does it work

HIFU means High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound. It reaches deeper skin layers and creates controlled thermal stimulation.

This process promotes:

- Initial tissue contraction.
- Progressive collagen activation.
- Gradual improvement in firmness and contour.

## Which areas can be treated

The most common areas include:

- Facial oval.
- Jawline.
- Double chin.
- Neck.
- Periocular area in selected protocols.

## How long until results are visible

You may notice early changes, but stronger results usually appear between weeks 8 and 12 due to the biological collagen remodeling process.

## Advantages compared with other options

- No surgery required.
- No prolonged downtime.
- Can be combined with an integral rejuvenation plan.

That said, it does not replace surgery when skin laxity is advanced. A proper evaluation prevents unrealistic expectations.

## Smart HIFU combinations

In practice, HIFU is often integrated with other strategies to optimize outcomes, such as dermocosmetic routines or complementary facial protocols.
