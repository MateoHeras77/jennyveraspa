## Carbon Peel with Laser: The Hollywood Peel Revolution

Looking for a solution to deeply cleanse your skin, reduce pores, and achieve that youthful glow without surgery? The **laser carbon peel treatment**, also known as the **Hollywood Peel**, is your answer.

This innovative procedure combines two powerful technologies: activated carbon (which penetrates and cleanses) and laser energy (which rejuvenates). The result is transformed, radiant, and revitalized skin in a matter of sessions.

At **Jenny Vera Spa**, we offer this cutting-edge treatment to our clients in Cuenca who want spectacular results without downtime.

## What is Laser Carbon Peel Treatment?

The Hollywood Peel is a non-invasive procedure that combines two elements:

### Activated Carbon

Activated carbon is an exceptional cleansing agent that:
- Penetrates deeply into pores
- Absorbs impurities, toxins, and excess oil
- Acts as a "magnet" for contaminants
- Prepares skin for laser energy

### Laser Technology

The laser (typically fractional CO2 or similar technology) then:
- Vaporizes the activated carbon
- Eliminates impurities along with superficial layers of dead skin
- Stimulates collagen production
- Promotes cellular regeneration

The net result: **deeply cleansed, rejuvenated, and radiant skin in a single session**.

This treatment is perfect if you have:

## Pre and Post-Treatment Care

### Before Treatment

- Avoid waxing 1-2 weeks before
- Avoid intense sun 5-7 days before
- Don't use retinol 3-5 days before
- Arrive with clean skin, no makeup
- Communicate medications you're taking

### After Treatment

- **First 24 hours**: 
  - Use only warm water for washing (no strong soaps)
  - Apply SPF 50+ every 2 hours if outside
  - Avoid activities that cause sweating
  - Don't wear makeup if possible

- **First 7 days**:
  - Use only gentle and soothing products
  - Avoid acids (AHAs, BHAs)
  - Avoid high-dose vitamin C
  - Keep skin hydrated
  - Avoid direct sun

- **Long term**:
  - Consistent sun protection routine
  - Maintain hydration
  - Follow-up with maintenance sessions
