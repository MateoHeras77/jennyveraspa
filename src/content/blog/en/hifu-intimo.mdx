## What is Intimate HIFU?

HIFU stands for **High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound**. In the intimate context:

### The Technology

- Uses high-frequency ultrasonic waves
- Focuses precisely on deep layers of skin
- NO incisions or general anesthesia required
- Stimulates collagen and elastin naturally
- The body absorbs the energy and regenerates

### How It Works

1. **Deep stimulation**: HIFU reaches deep layers of collagen
2. **Controlled micro-trauma**: Generates microscopic contraction points
3. **Natural regeneration**: The body responds with new collagen production
4. **Firmness and elasticity**: Result is firmer, more elastic, youthful tissue

It's like a "non-invasive lift" for the intimate area.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is it painful?

No, it's painless. Most describe a pleasant sensation of warmth. Completely tolerable without anesthesia.

### Is it safe?

Yes, HIFU has been approved by regulatory authorities for gynecological use. When performed by trained professionals (like at Jenny Vera Spa), it's very safe.

### Does sexual function get affected?

Actually, many women report improved sensitivity and satisfaction thanks to restored firmness.

### What happens if I don't do maintenance?

Results gradually diminish in 12-18 months. One annual session maintains benefits indefinitely.

### Will my partner notice?

The improvement in firmness is perceptible. Many women report their partners notice the positive difference.

### Can it be used during menstruation?

It's recommended to wait until menstruation ends for comfort, but it's not a strict contraindication. Consult with our team.

### Are there special post-treatment care requirements?

Minimal:
- Avoid sexual intercourse 48-72 hours
- Avoid intense hot baths 24 hours
- Avoid tampons for 48 hours
- Everything else is normal
