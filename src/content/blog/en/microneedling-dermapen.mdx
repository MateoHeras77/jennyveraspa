Microneedling is one of the most versatile and effective treatments in regenerative aesthetic medicine. Unlike chemical peels or lasers that destroy tissue, microneedling stimulates your own skin to repair itself and produce new collagen. The result: real renewal, without aggression.

At Jenny Vera Spa, we use Dermapen technology, considered the gold standard in microneedling for its precision and efficacy. Many patients are surprised by how it transforms their skin in just a few sessions.

## What is microneedling

Microneedling is a procedure that uses ultrafine needles (between 0.5mm and 2.5mm depth, depending on need) to create controlled micro-lesions in the skin. These "tiny wounds" are deliberate: they stimulate your body's natural repair system.

### Dermapen: cutting-edge technology

Dermapen is a motorized device containing a cartridge with multiple needles (between 12 and 36 depending on the model). It vibrates rapidly while gliding across your skin, creating thousands of uniform, controlled micro-channels.

Unlike manual rollers (which are less precise), Dermapen allows you to adjust depth, speed, and coverage. That's why results are predictable and superior.

## How it works biologically

The mechanism is pure biological survival instinct:

### 1. Controlled creation of micro-lesions

The needles open thousands of microscopic channels in the epidermis and dermis. Your body interprets this as a "wound" that needs repair.

### 2. Fibroblast activation

Immediately, cells called fibroblasts "wake up" and begin synthesizing collagen and elastin to "close" those micro-lesions.

### 3. Collagen production

For weeks after treatment, new collagen is produced. This process is called "collagenesis" and is the mechanism by which skin truly rejuvenates.

### 4. Progressive remodeling

Unlike a peel that sees immediate results, microneedling provides progressive improvement for 8-12 weeks post-treatment. Your skin looks better and better.

### 5. Increased permeability

The open micro-channels allow topical actives to penetrate 100-200 times deeper. That's why combining with [PDRN](/blog/pdrn-salmon-rejuvenecimiento-celular), [exosomes](/blog/exosomas-rejuvenecimiento), or specialized serums is ideal.

## What it treats (complete indications)

Microneedling is versatile. It works well for almost all rejuvenation indications:

### Fine lines and moderate wrinkles
Probably best for fine wrinkles. The new collagen produced naturally smooths and fills.

### Acne scars
Works especially well on "ice pick" scars or depressions. New collagen fibers gradually elevate the scar.

### Irregular texture and dilated pores
By stimulating collagen, pores naturally contract and texture evens out.

### Stretch marks
Doesn't eliminate them completely, but significantly improves texture and appearance, especially recent ones.

### Dark spots and discoloration
When combined with depigmenting serums, it greatly potentiates their effect.

### Post-procedure scarring
Accelerates healing after laser, peeling, or other treatments.

### Loss of firmness
Stimulates elastin and collagen, improving overall tone.

### Dull, tired-looking skin
The natural repair process gives luminosity and vitality.

## The procedure step by step

### 1. Consultation and prior evaluation

We assess your skin, specific indications, and expectations. We define needle depth (0.5mm to 2.5mm depending on the issue).

### 2. Preparation

Gentle cleansing is performed. Optionally, topical anesthesia is applied (for comfort, though many feel fine without it).

### 3. Dermapen session

The specialist moves the Dermapen in systematic patterns across the entire area (face, neck, décolletage, or specific area).
- Time: 20-30 minutes
- Sensation: small pricks/light scratching (tolerable)
- No significant pain

### 4. Post-procedure application

Immediately after, calming serum or mask is applied. Some specialists apply [PDRN](/blog/pdrn-salmon-rejuvenecimiento-celular) or [exosomes](/blog/exosomas-rejuvenecimiento) at this moment to maximize penetration.

### 5. Protection and care

Mandatory sun protection and specific care instructions are provided.

## Recovery and post-treatment care

Recovery is quick but requires discipline:

### First 24 hours
- Redness (normal, like mild sun exposure)
- Possible slight swelling
- Increased sensitivity

### Days 2-7
- Redness gradually decreases
- Important: don't "pull" skin (no exfoliating)
- Intensive hydration
- Mandatory sun protection (SPF 50+)

### Mandatory care

- **Avoid direct sun** for at least 1 week
- **Don't exfoliate** for 2 weeks
- **Avoid retinoids or vitamin C** for 1 week (too much stimulation)
- **Deep hydration:** masks and calming serums
- **Sun protection** is non-negotiable

### Optional care that enhances results

- Apply topical [PDRN](/blog/pdrn-salmon-rejuvenecimiento-celular) on days 2-4
- Use serums with niacinamide or centella asiatica
- Take marine collagen (some studies suggest it accelerates endogenous production)

## How often to do microneedling

This is where discipline comes in. Your skin needs time to repair:

- **Minimum frequency:** 4 weeks between sessions
- **Optimal frequency:** 6-8 weeks between sessions (allows complete collagen cycle)
- **Never:** Less than 3 weeks (you can create cumulative damage)

Some want to do it weekly. Don't. Your skin doesn't produce collagen that fast. Patience is key.

## Contraindications (when NOT to do it)

- Active infections (herpes, bacterial)
- Severe rosacea (requires special management)
- Extremely sensitive skin (consider shallower depths)
- Keloid scarring (high risk of complication)
- Pregnancy (precaution, though not absolute contraindication)
- Recent aggressive procedures (wait minimum 2 weeks)
